Research Experience & Interests

Since I was a child, I have always wanted to be an astronaut, so science was always something I gravitated towards. Outreach

The greatest teachers that I have ever had in my life had the uncanny ability to fascinate their students regardless of the subject. Unfortunately, the opposite situation is more common, and many students, young and old, miss out on an otherwise incredible opportunity which may spark a lifelong passion. For this reason, I emphasize the importance and need for great education for children and strive to generate interest in science and math through STEM outreach. Until COVID, I had continuously volunteered in some way to provide resources to the people around me. This includes volunteering at a local science museum and tutoring children K-12 in math and physics. This Fall I am planning on volunteering as a Wizard of Wright (WOW), a group which brings flashy and engaging science demonstrations to classrooms in the Dayton, OH area.
